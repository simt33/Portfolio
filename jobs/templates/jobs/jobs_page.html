Communication

  • Skilled in building and maintaining positive relationships; highly collaborative
  • Excellent presentation skills (verbal and visual)

  • Fluent in French; Strong ability in Spanish
  • Ability to convey technical/complex information to non-technical audience

Problem-solving

I excel in breaking down complex problems or concepts, spotting the problems or difficulties, and then rebuilding it better. As a teacher, this was how I was able to convey complex linguistic ideas to students; as a data manager I have used these skills to identify and improve data systems in collaboration with senior managers.

  • A natural innovator with extensive problem-solving skills
  • Highly inquisitive, and motivated by the ‘why’

  • A history of technical problem-solving, especially within data systems

Learning

I love to learn! It’s one of the things that attracts me to technology so much, and the main reason I originally entered education. I believe that teachers make the best learners, and this has helped me to become very adept at broadening and honing my technical knowledge in a relatively short time.

  • Highly adaptable self-starter and a proactive learner
  • Very quick in the acquisition of new skills and technologies
  • T-shaped – I like to know a lot about my specialist areas, but also understand the larger landscape
