A highly collaborative, proactive and adaptable individual with a broad applied skillset across technology, language and music. Highly proficient in a number of data and development technologies, speaks three languages, plays 6 instruments, with degrees from Durham and Warwick Universities and a successful career history spanning education, recruitment and data, both in the UK and abroad. Having spent the last 10 years seeking out data problems alongside his primary roles, Simon is now looking for a new fully data-centric position as a data analyst or engineer, where he can solely focus on adding business value through improving data systems.
